Before You Begin

Important information about the assessment

Time Required

This assessment contains 10 questions and typically takes 3-5 minutes to complete. You can save your progress and return later if needed.

How to Answer

Each question uses a 7-point scale from "Strongly Agree" (1) to "Strongly Disagree" (7). Choose the response that best reflects your typical behavior or feelings.

Be Honest

There are no right or wrong answers. Answer honestly based on who you are, not who you'd like to be. Your first instinct is usually the most accurate.

Privacy & Confidentiality

Your responses are completely confidential and will only be used to generate your personality profile. Results are private and secure.

Progress Saving

Your progress is automatically saved. If you need to pause, you can return later and continue from where you left off.
